Welcome to TCET

Vision
Thakur College of Engineering and Technology will excel in Technical Education to become an internationally renowned premier Institute of Engineering and Technology. The Institute is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) & Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to the University of Mumbai (UOM).
Mission
To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure and right academic ambience for developing professional skills as well as an environment for growth of leadership and managerial skills to students which will make them competent engineers to deliver quality results in industry. TCET offers 15 UG (8 in Engineering, 3 in Technology and 4 in Vocational Course), 3 PG and 3 Ph.D. (Tech.) programmes.
